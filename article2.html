Why I Started Building a Fitness App as My First Web Project

Combining my two passions — coding and fitness — led me to start building a fitness tracking app as my very first web project. This journey taught me more than just programming syntax; it gave me hands-on experience with user experience design, problem-solving, and the discipline needed to see a project through.

In this article, I break down how merging fitness goals with coding challenges helped me understand user needs better. I talk about the hurdles I faced, from designing workout logging features to making the app intuitive for beginners.

If you’re thinking about your first project or want to build something meaningful, this article offers insights on how to stay motivated, learn new skills, and deliver value to users from day one.
